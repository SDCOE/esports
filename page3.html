ESports Field Trips

The San Diego County Office of Education Innovation Division is partnering with the North American Scholastic ESports Federation hosted by the Orange County Department of Education (OCDE) to bring ESports to San Diego County.

This is an exciting partnership. SDCOE co-hosted a field trip in mid October 2019 to a school site in Orange County that has successfully integrated esports into their curriculum while running a successful esports club. Guests visited classrooms to see how teachers have integrated the A-G esports curriculum.

Females in Gaming

Dec. 20
Microsoft Store

Women are influencing the gaming industry. The San Diego County Office of Education partnered with the Microsoft Store in Fashion Valley Mall to host our first Female esports event geared towards San Diego County female gamers. The night was an opportunity to Network with females in the gaming community and learn from the experiences of female gamers, creators and influencers.
